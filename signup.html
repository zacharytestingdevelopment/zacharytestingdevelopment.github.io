What's in the box?

FLEXIBLE TOOLKIT
Customize everything to fit the needs of every client. Save your favorite configurations and access them anywhere, anytime on your phone, tablet, computer, and more.
RESULTS TRACKING
Keeping track of client progress is an essential part of helping them heal. Say goodbye to cluttered paperwork with our powerful data tracking and visualization services.
ACCESSIBLE FEATURES
Nobody should be barred from participating in EMDR because of things they cannot control. That's why we offer auditory as well as visual stimulus so that everybody is taken care of, including the physically disabled.
