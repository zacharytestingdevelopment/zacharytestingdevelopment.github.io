What is EMDR?
( Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing )
EMDR is a groundbreaking therapy used to treat PTSD, Anxiety, OCD, and other trauma related disorders. Its efficacy has been demonstrated time and time again through clinical trials, with some finding it to have a long term success rate comparable with, or better than, traditional methods such as drug therapy and cognitive behavioral therapy.
The current tools required to perform EMDR can cost hundreds or even thousands of dollars just to get started. We offer a more personal and powerful solution that allows you to get started at minimal cost — welcome to the future of EMDR.
